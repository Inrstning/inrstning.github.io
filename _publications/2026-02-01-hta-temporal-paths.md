**Role:** First author. **Status:** Under review.

We propose **HTA**, a hybrid Transformer architecture for temporal knowledge graph forecasting:

- Builds **multi-hop reasoning paths** with fine-grained time encoding.
- Designs an **Attention-Mixer** module that fuses self-attention with MLP for complementary token mixing.

On four public benchmarks HTA reaches **SOTA**, with a **>20% MRR improvement on GDELT**, substantially mitigating the performance degradation caused by sparse long-range dependencies.
