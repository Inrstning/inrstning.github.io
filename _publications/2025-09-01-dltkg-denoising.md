**Role:** Co-author.

We propose **DLTKG**, a temporal knowledge graph reasoning framework that:

- Models reasoning as a **diffusion-style noise injection + denoising** process.
- Conditions the denoising trajectory on **Historical Evolution Paths (HEPs)** of relations.
- Introduces a **memory-fusion strategy** that fuses historical entities into fuzzy entities.

The design simultaneously improves **generalization to unseen facts** and the **interpretability** of the reasoning process.
